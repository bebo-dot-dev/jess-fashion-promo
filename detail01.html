The Great Divide

I created this collage with the article https://www.dazeddigital.com/fashion/article/40206/1/what-fashion-needs-to-understand-about-being-gender-neutral in mind, considering the ‘divide’ between what is considered masculine & feminine. I want to focus on typically feminine traits & design elements in an attempt to prove that female signifiers can and should be considered gender neutral.
