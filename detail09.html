Styled Photoshoot

This is a small collection of images I took for a photoshoot inspired by my research around the topic of gender. I chose this model as he has quite a masculine facial structure, he is tall and has broad shoulders. I thought this would be interesting to contrast against a feminine styling and would create a more androgynous outcome.

Inspired by my research into historical fashion, I styled the model with lace; a parasol & gloves, and a pearl necklace. I also applied some dark eyeshadow to reference my research into punk.

I love the way this image looks – the composition of the flowers in the foreground and the model’s position creates a vulnerable image. I used two locations; a lounge and a garden. I wanted to shoot in the lounge to explore ideas of femininity in the domestic sphere, and the garden to create images derivative of pre-raphaelite art.
