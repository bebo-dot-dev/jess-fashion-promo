Brand Research

I looked at some of the brands mentioned in the inital article to research what design elements are considered gender neutral. I noted that the problem with modern fashion is the lack of gender neutral clothes available that still maintain an element of femininity – essentially, most ‘gender neutral’ clothes could easily be marketed in the men’s section. This is why I have chosen to focus on femininity, and how it can be expressed from a gender neutral & androgynous perspective.
