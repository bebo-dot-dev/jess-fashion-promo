Joie de Vivre

I found this editorial photoshoot entitled ‘Joie de Vivre’ in the March 2019 issue of Vogue. I was inspired by this shoot to look at gender within renaissance art & historical fashion as i love the decadance and luxury exhibited in this era of fashion. I like the combination of textures, deep, rich tones of fabrics and extravagant accessories.
