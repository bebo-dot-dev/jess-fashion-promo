Only Anarchists are Pretty

Following my research into various gender neutral clothing lines, I was inspired by an editorial entitled ‘Deviating from Gender’ which seemed to me derivative of punk fashion & Vivienne Westwood. I created this collage to explore Westwood’s ideas about beauty & rebellion via fashion.
