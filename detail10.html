Styled Photoshoot

I used the swing as it reminded me of the beautiful painting by Jean-Honoré Fragonard, ‘The Swing’. Something about this setting made me think of Victorian elegance & wealth, which is why I chose to shoot here.

I like the composition of the model looking through the frame of the swing as there are lots of interesting leading lines drawing the viewer’s eye to the subject.

I also edited the outdoor images slightly – I adjusted the saturation and added a pinkish layer to make the series of images cohesive.
