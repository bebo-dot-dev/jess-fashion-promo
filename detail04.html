Androgyny does not equal Masculinity

Linking to the article I read, I wanted to create a collage that created a distinction between ‘gender neutral’ and “without any traditional female signifiers” – Jaimie Wylie. I intentionally used a model that is styled feminine yet also androgynous to reinforce this point. I also used lettering that is similar to the Sex Pistols classic font as this links to Vivienne Westwood, punk & rebellion.
