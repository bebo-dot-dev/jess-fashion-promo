Femininity in the Pre-Raphaelites vs. Modern Fashion

I collated images of paintings that present classic pre-raphaelite femininity and contrasted them with a more modern presentation within fashion. An interesting thing I noticed is that when it comes to typical modern femininity, not much has changed. However, models are often more diverse in terms of race, facial structure and hair length/colour. Another stark difference is obviously the clothes. My favourite part about femininity in the pre-raphaelites is the overwhelming presence of the natural world & flowers – I would like to develop this further in my own work.
